Ways to Increase Your Confidence During an Interview

Author: Raden Gheryl Ivan Mahardika


For those of you who receive a job interview call, you will certainly feel worried and nervous, this condition is normal and experienced by many candidates. Nervousness or tension can be caused by several factors, such as feeling less experienced in the field of work you are applying for and therefore feeling less confident.


Confidence is very important, you know, don't let the interview moment become a mess just because you lack confidence. Appearing confident during an interview will convince the company to recruit candidates. Those who appear confident are believed to have better performance at work. Therefore, self-confidence is one of the keys to success in passing a job interview.

Before carrying out a job interview, make sure to appear and look confident in front of the recruiter. You must be able to show this confidence with thorough preparation, so that recruiters believe you have the abilities needed by the company.

                                         Causes of Not Confidence During an Interview 
                        
Lack of confidence during a job interview can be influenced by various factors. Lack of self-confidence can be seen from feeling nervous, tense, and even worried during the interview. If you have no experience at all in the world of work, of course this condition will make your self-confidence decrease.
                        
Apart from that, the cause of lack of self-confidence arises when the job position you are applying for does not match the qualifications you have. Fear and worry arise regarding the questions that will arise during the interview process. This condition makes you less confident in your abilities.
                        
Lack of confidence during a job interview can also be caused by a lack of preparation. Lack of preparation will make you feel difficult and afraid of making mistakes when answering recruiter questions.
                        
                        Effective Ways to Increase Self-Confidence During an Interview
                        
1. Using breathing techniques
                        
    You must appear relaxed during a job interview. A relaxed and relaxed condition will make you more focused and calmer in answering each question. The way to stay calm during a job interview can be by using breathing techniques. Breathing techniques also help you when you are stressed, anxious, worried and panicked. The method is to sit in the most comfortable position, inhale slowly from your nose, exhale slowly from your mouth.
                        
2. Look neat
                        
    One factor in lack of self-confidence can be caused by appearance, you know. You will feel less confident if your appearance, especially the clothes you wear, are not neat. Dressing appropriately is an important part of appearing confident during a job interview. Try to wear neat clothes when attending a job interview.
                        
3. Prepare interview materials
                        
    The way to increase your self-confidence during an interview is by preparing interview material that is often asked by interviewers. You can also make notes on material that can be studied, also include answers that are commonly known, for example work experience, about the company you are applying for and many other general questions.
                        
4. Do Interview Practice
                        
    One way to appear confident is to prepare yourself well. The way to do this is by doing interview practice and preparing interview material that is frequently asked. You can practice in front of the mirror and practice answers that are often asked by recruiters. You can look for information related to questions that are often asked during interviews.
                        
    Apart from general questions, you can prepare questions related to the company profile, including vision and mission, products, history and work culture. You can search for it on websites, social media and even ask workers at the company directly. Thorough preparation will make you more confident and confident during the interview.
                        
5. Pay attention to body language
                        
    It is very important to pay attention to body posture during an interview so that you appear confident. Make sure you sit up straight and straighten your legs so you look more ready than in a hunched position. Apart from that, sitting up straight also helps you be clearer when speaking.
                        
    Show enthusiastic body gestures, don't forget to smile to increase your self-confidence because you are one of the candidates who could be worthy of filling this position.
                        
6. Focus on your strengths and the best career you have
                        
    Remembering your own shortcomings will create a feeling of lack of self-confidence. You can start practicing to focus on your strengths and best career experiences to increase your self-confidence.
                        
    Many companies want to recruit candidates with the best potential. Therefore, you must convincingly demonstrate the advantages you have. Focus on your strengths, make the advantages you have the main provision and make the company see the extraordinary potential in you.
                        
7. Maintain body condition
                        
    Some people may have habits which is not good for health, such as staying up late. This condition will have a direct impact during the job interview. It cannot be denied that poor sleep quality will affect your health condition. Sleeping late and not getting enough rest will make you look less enthusiastic and less confident during the interview.
                        
    When undergoing a job interview, the abilities and skills you have will appear useless if they are not accompanied by self-confidence. Confidence will make you appear more convincing in front of the interviewer. Confidence will help you show your value, answer questions clearly, and show your best potential.
